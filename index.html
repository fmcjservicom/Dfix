About Us

Terms of Service

1. Acceptance of Terms

By accessing and using this platform, you agree to these terms of service.

2. Services

We provide instant airtime recharge, data bundle purchase, and recharge card printing services across all Nigerian mobile networks.

3. Wallet & Payments

Funds added to your wallet are non-refundable except in cases of failed transactions, which are automatically refunded. All payments are processed securely via Paystack.

4. User Responsibilities

Users must provide accurate phone numbers for transactions. We are not liable for transactions sent to wrong numbers due to user error.

5. Transaction Policy

All transactions are processed instantly. Failed transactions are automatically refunded within minutes. If your refund is delayed, please contact support.

6. Account Security

Users are responsible for keeping their login credentials secure. Report any unauthorized access immediately.

7. Modifications

We reserve the right to modify these terms at any time. Continued use constitutes acceptance of modified terms.

Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

We collect your name, email, phone number, and transaction history to provide our services.

How We Use Your Data

Your information is used solely for processing transactions, account management, and customer support. We never sell your data to third parties.

Data Security

All passwords are encrypted using SHA-256 hashing. Payment processing is handled by Paystack, a PCI-DSS compliant payment processor.

Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser local storage to maintain your login session. No tracking cookies are used.

Your Rights

You can request deletion of your account and data at any time by contacting our support team.

FAQ

How do I fund my wallet?
Click on "Fund Wallet" from the dashboard or profile page. Enter the amount and pay securely via Paystack. Your wallet is credited instantly upon successful payment.
What happens if my transaction fails?
Failed transactions are automatically refunded to your wallet. If your refund is delayed, please contact us via WhatsApp support.
How does the referral program work?
Share your unique referral code with friends. When they register using your code, you earn a referral bonus directly to your wallet.
What is the minimum wallet funding?
The minimum wallet funding amount is ₦100.
How do I print recharge cards?
Go to "Print Recharge Card", select the network, denomination, and quantity. PINs are generated instantly and you can download them as a professional PDF voucher.
Can I add my business name to vouchers?
Yes! When printing recharge cards, you can enter your own business name. If left blank, our site name will appear on the vouchers.
